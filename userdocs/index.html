LinkUp User Guide

LinkUp is an AI-powered productivity app that helps users prepare for meetings, generate professional email responses, and create follow-up messages quickly and confidently.

This guide covers the core flow for students, professionals, and job seekers.

Key Features

  • AI-generated meeting preparation
  • Instant email response generation
  • Automated follow-up emails
  • Simple onboarding and profile setup
  • Clean and easy-to-use interface

Getting Started

Step 1: Sign Up / Log In

  1. Open the app.
  2. Enter your email and password.
  3. Tap Sign Up or Log In.

Step 2: Complete Onboarding

  1. Enter your name.
  2. Select your career interest.
  3. Tap Complete Profile.

Step 3: Set Up Profile

  • Add or edit your name.
  • Update your career interest.
  • Upload a profile picture.

How to Use the App

Meeting Preparation

  1. Navigate to Meeting Prep.
  2. Enter interviewer name, company name, and details.
  3. Tap Generate Meeting Prep.

Email Responses

  1. Go to Email Responses.
  2. Enter context or choose an option.
  3. Tap Generate Email Response.

Follow-Up Emails

  1. Open Follow-Up Email.
  2. Enter company and interviewer name.
  3. Tap Generate Follow-up Email.
