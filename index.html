About this app

Designed around the full networking workflow.

LinkUp supports users from the moment they schedule a conversation through the follow-up process. The goal is to reduce preparation time while helping users stay organized and professional.

Meeting prep briefs

Create concise notes with company context, role-specific talking points, and tailored questions.

Contact organization

Keep meeting details and networking contacts in one organized place.

Follow-up drafting

Turn meeting notes into a professional follow-up message after the conversation.
